# Meridian Technologies — Employee Handbook

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Version 3.2

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

### Accrual Rates

All full-time employees accrue PTO based on tenure:

- **0–2 years**: 15 days per year (1.25 days/month)
- **3–5 years**: 20 days per year (1.67 days/month)
- **6–10 years**: 25 days per year (2.08 days/month)
- **10+ years**: 30 days per year (2.5 days/month)

Part-time employees accrue PTO on a prorated basis relative to their scheduled hours.

### Requesting PTO

Submit PTO requests through the Workday portal at least 5 business days in advance for planned absences. Requests of 5 or more consecutive days require 15 business days notice. Emergency or sick leave should be reported to your direct manager by 9:00 AM on the day of absence.

### Carryover Policy

Employees may carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the next calendar year. Any days beyond 5 will be forfeited on January 1. Employees departing the company will be paid out for unused PTO up to a maximum of 10 days.

### Company Holidays

Meridian observes 11 paid holidays per year: New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Columbus Day, Veterans Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day. When a holiday falls on a weekend, the nearest weekday is observed.

## Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility

All employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period are eligible to work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements require VP-level approval and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

### Core Hours

Remote employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local time zone. Outside of core hours, employees may set flexible schedules with manager approval.

### Home Office Requirements

Employees working remotely must maintain a dedicated workspace with reliable internet (minimum 25 Mbps download, 5 Mbps upload). The company provides a one-time $500 home office stipend for new remote-eligible employees. After the first year, employees may request up to $200 annually for equipment replacement or upgrades.

### In-Office Days

Teams are expected to coordinate at least 2 shared in-office days per week. Department heads set the specific days for their teams. All-hands meetings on the first Monday of each month require in-person attendance unless a remote exception has been granted.

## Expense Reimbursement

### Eligible Expenses

The company reimburses reasonable business expenses including: travel (airfare, hotel, ground transportation), client meals, conference registration fees, professional development courses, and pre-approved software subscriptions.

### Spending Limits

- **Meals**: Up to $75 per person for client dinners, $25 per person for team lunches
- **Hotels**: Up to $250/night for domestic travel, $350/night for international
- **Flights**: Economy class for flights under 6 hours, premium economy for 6+ hours
- **Professional development**: Up to $2,500 per year per employee

### Submission Process

Submit expense reports through Concur within 30 days of the expense. Include itemized receipts for all expenses over $25. Manager approval is required for all reports; expenses over $1,000 also require Finance department approval.

### Reimbursement Timeline

Approved expenses are reimbursed within 10 business days via direct deposit. International currency expenses are converted at the exchange rate on the date of the transaction.

## Performance Reviews

### Review Cycle

Meridian conducts formal performance reviews twice per year: mid-year (June) and end-of-year (December). Managers are expected to hold monthly 1:1 check-ins with each direct report.

### Rating Scale

Performance is rated on a 5-point scale:
1. **Needs Improvement** — Not meeting expectations; performance improvement plan required
2. **Developing** — Meeting some expectations but growth needed in key areas
3. **Meeting Expectations** — Consistently delivering solid work
4. **Exceeding Expectations** — Going above and beyond in multiple areas
5. **Outstanding** — Exceptional performance; top 10% of peer group

### Compensation Adjustments

Annual merit increases are tied to performance ratings and are effective March 1. The typical merit pool is 3–5% of base salary. Employees rated "Outstanding" may receive an additional spot bonus of up to 10% of base salary.

## Benefits

### Health Insurance

Meridian offers three medical plan options through Aetna: Bronze (employee-only: $85/month), Silver (employee + dependents: $220/month), and Gold (comprehensive family: $380/month). Dental and vision are included in Silver and Gold plans. Coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date.

### 401(k) Retirement Plan

Employees are eligible for the 401(k) plan after 60 days of employment. Meridian matches 100% of contributions up to 4% of salary and 50% of the next 2% (maximum company match: 5% of salary). Vesting follows a 3-year graduated schedule: 33% after year 1, 66% after year 2, 100% after year 3.

### Parental Leave

Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 6 weeks of paid leave. Parental leave must be taken within 12 months of the birth or adoption of a child.
